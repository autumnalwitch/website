About Me

Hi, I’m Autumn! I’m a recent graduate from the University of Birmingham, with a 2:1 (Hons) Masters degree in Theoretical Physics. My research background includes quantum mechanics, group & representation theory, and topological approaches to physics.

My recent activities explore geometric representations of quantum spin and connections to other areas of mathematical science such as random matrix theory and electromagnetism in 2 dimensions. I also have experience working with mathematical coherent states and other topological systems such as knot theory.
